# Enterprise DBA Estate Operations Framework

This framework is designed for large estates managed by multi-team DBA organizations.

## Domains

1. Governance & operating model
2. Fleet inventory & ownership
3. Observability standards and SLOs
4. Incident management and escalation
5. Change/release controls
6. Backup/restore governance
7. HA/DR strategy and drills
8. Security/compliance controls
9. Performance engineering lifecycle
10. Capacity and FinOps
11. Automation and orchestration
12. Data lifecycle (archive/purge)
13. NoSQL operating standards
14. Team enablement and training

Use these folders as the system-of-record for daily operations, audit readiness, and automation adoption.
