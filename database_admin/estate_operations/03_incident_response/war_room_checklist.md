# War Room Checklist

1. Declare incident + assign incident commander.
2. Freeze risky changes.
3. Collect health snapshots (CPU, waits, locks, replication lag).
4. Decide mitigation path (throttle, failover, rollback, scale).
5. Capture timeline and decisions.
6. Validate recovery with synthetic and business checks.
7. Start RCA document within 24h.
